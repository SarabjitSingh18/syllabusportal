Engineering Chemistry

B.Tech Electronics & Communication Engineering
Guru Nanak Dev University
Total Lectures: 45

SECTION A

  • Water hardness: Common impurities, EDTA method, degree of hardness, numerical problems. (4 Lectures)
  • Water hardness related problems: Boiler troubles, corrosion, caustic embrittlement. (2 Lectures)
  • Water treatment techniques: Purification, coagulation, filtration, sterilization. (3 Lectures)
  • Softening of water: Lime-Soda, Zeolite, Deionization methods. (3 Lectures)

SECTION B

  • Glasses, Ceramics, Composites: Glassy state, manufacturing, applications. (6 Lectures)
  • Magnetic Materials: Types, hard & soft magnetic materials. (3 Lectures)
  • Refractories: Classification, silica & fire clay refractories. (3 Lectures)

SECTION C

  • Polymers: Classification, properties, synthesis of polymers. (6 Lectures)
  • Polymer processing methods: Injection, Compression, Blow film, Extrusion. (3 Lectures)
  • Rubber: Natural & synthetic rubbers, applications. (3 Lectures)

SECTION D

  • Silicone compounds: Preparation, properties, applications. (2 Lectures)
  • Electrochemistry: Cells, Faraday’s laws, electrode potential. (4 Lectures)
  • Nanomaterials: CNTs, graphene, applications. (3 Lectures)
